About The Event

JGAM 2025 organised in conjunction with the Diamond Jubilee Celebrations of JNTUHUCESTH brings together the global alumni community of JNTUH.

This grand reunion offers a platform to reconnect with batchmates, revisit memories, and engage with the University's progress and vision.

With interactive sessions, cultural programs and networking opportunities, JGAM 2025 is more than a celebration- it's a tribute to the shared journey of our vibrant alumni community.
